Designed and implemented a fire detection system utilizing sensors and machine learning algorithms to detect and alert potential fire hazard

Project Date April 15, 2022
Role Identity, hardware integration
Client College
For More Details Visit Fire Detection System

DISCLAIMER : The prototype is currently miniaturized with mini components like MQ -6 gas sensor, arduino UNO board, buzzer,300 RPM ,12 V Centre Shaft Gear Motor ,L298N Motor Driver etc.


How it Works


IDEAL : The prototype activates when gas leakage is detected, triggering a motor and buzzer by the machine learning algorithms. The buzzer alerts people outside, and then the motor activates a shielding mechanism to close doors, windows, and roof tops with inert gas-coated shields.

When outside gas levels decrease, the motor reverses, retracting the shields. Oxygen bottles are provided indoors to prevent suffocation, commonly used in airplanes and by high-altitude trekkers to maintain oxygen levels.
